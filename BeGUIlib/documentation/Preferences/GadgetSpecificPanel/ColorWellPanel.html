The ColorWell Color Preference Panel

    This preference panel gives control of the ColorWell class's colors (the View color, the High color and the Low color). Changing the View color changes the deepest part of the well's color. Changing the High color changes the sides (background) of the well. The Low color has no effect, but should be made to control the color spread between the sides of the well and the depths of the well. Currently a very simple algorithm is used and needs to be improved upon!
