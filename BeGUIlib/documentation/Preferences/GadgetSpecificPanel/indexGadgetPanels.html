    An Example Panel

    These are the various specific gadget control panels. They are are very similar. Each has access to a View, High and Low color control gadget which allows the color customization of each class of gadgets. All but the primary underlying view's panels have representative gadgets which show the effect of your choice in real-time (I may soon add a similar demonstration area to the underlying view panel). Some panels may have specific extra controls, like the MyStatusBar's individual Bar height Control. The example panel gives you a good idea of how things look.
