The StringDrawer Color Preference Panel

    This preference panel gives control of the StringDrawer class's colors (the View color, the High color and the Low color). This class is used to put textual information into a window. You have complete control over the font in your program. Changing the View color has no obvious affect. Changing the High color sets the primary drawing color of the string. The Low color affects the anti-aliasing and should be a color somewhat darker than the High color of the StringDrawer in the direction (if colors can be said to have a direction) of the Underlying View, if that color is darker than the primary drawing color. If the Underlying View is lighter, the low color should be lighter, tending toward it. This is a bit complicated, so play about with these things a bit to get the idea!
