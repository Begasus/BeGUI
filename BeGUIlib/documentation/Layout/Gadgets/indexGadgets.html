These are the gadgets currently supported by BeGUI. The BSlider class and the BeTab and BTabView classes are not quite ready for inclusion at this time. I tacked on a "My" and removed the "B" from the BeOS class names where the gadgets are basically identical. The other gadgets use BeOS Interface objects in specific ways, or in ways not generally supported by the normal BeOS classes.
